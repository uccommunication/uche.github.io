Savings Account

A savings account is designed for individuals to deposit and save money while earning interest. It usually provides a lower interest rate compared to other investment options but offers easy access to funds. Savings accounts are a common choice for short-term financial goals and emergency funds.

Checking Account

A checking account is a transactional account that allows frequent deposits and withdrawals. It often comes with features such as a debit card, check-writing privileges, and online banking. Checking accounts are suitable for everyday expenses, bill payments, and managing daily transactions.

Swiftbruxel

Certificate of Deposit (CD)

A Certificate of Deposit is a time deposit account with a fixed term and interest rate. Customers deposit a specific amount for a predetermined period, and in return, they receive higher interest rates than regular savings accounts. CDs are suitable for individuals looking for a secure, low-risk investment with a fixed return over a set period.
